Upload company details

When you submit your organisations’ packaging data to the environmental regulator, you also need to give their company details.

This includes things like:

  • address
  • turnover
  • packaging activities

Other information you may need to give

Some organisations also need to report their:

  • brand activities - if they supply goods to the UK market in primary, secondary, or shipment packaging under their own brand
  • partners - if they’re registered as a partnership in Companies House

What you need to do

  1. Read the guidance on company details you need to collect and submit to report packaging data (opens in a new window)..
  2. Upload the company details in a CSV file.
  3. Based on what is in the company details file, you may need to also upload separate CSV files with information about brands and partners.

Once the files are uploaded an authorised person must submit them to the environmental regulator. There is a separate step to do that.

The information you submit needs to be true on the deadline of 1 January 1900.
