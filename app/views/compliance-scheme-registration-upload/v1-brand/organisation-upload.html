Upload organisation details

When you submit an organisation’s packaging data you also need to report other details.

Organisation details include things like:

  • address
  • turnover
  • packaging activities

You may also need to report an organisation's:

  • brand activities - if they supply goods to the UK market in primary, secondary, or shipment packaging under their own brand
  • partners - if they’re registered as a partnership in Companies House

Once you submit these details, the organisation is officially registered for the reporting cycle.

What you need to do

  1. Read the guidance on how to report organisation details (opens in a new window).
  2. Upload the company details in a CSV file, or using the Excel template in the guidance.
  3. Based on what's in the company details file, you may need to also upload separate files with information about brands and partners.
  4. Submit the files to the regulator by 1 October 2023. Only people with the right account permission can do this.
