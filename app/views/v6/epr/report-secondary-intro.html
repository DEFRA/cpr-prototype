Report your organisation's secondary packaging for 2022

Secondary packaging is conceived so as to constitute at the point of purchase a grouping of a certain number of sales units whether the latter is sold as such to the final user or consumer or whether it serves only as a means to replenish the shelves at the point of sale; it can be removed from the product without affecting its characteristics.

The secondary packaging is the cardboard trays and the plastic wrap.
