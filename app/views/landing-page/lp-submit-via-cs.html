Compliance scheme: Acme Org Scotland

What Acme Org Scotland is responsible for

On behalf of Kite Packaging, Acme Org Scotland is responsible for:

  • submitting packaging data to the environmental regulator
  • reporting organisation details and keeping them up to date
  • handling packaging recovery notes (PRNs) - these are evidence that packaging has been recycled, recovered or exported
  • paying scheme administrator costs and charges to the environmental regulator

What Kite Packaging is responsible for

Kite Packaging is responsible for any waste management fee that may apply to it.
