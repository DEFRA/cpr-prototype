What Valpak Scotland is responsible for

On behalf of Acme Org, Valpak Scotland is responsible for:

  • submitting packaging data to the environmental regulator
  • reporting organisation details and keeping them up to date
  • getting packaging recovery notes (PRNs) - these are evidence that packaging has been recycled, recovered or exported

Valpak Scotland is responsible for paying the waste management fee.
