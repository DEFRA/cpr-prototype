Compliance scheme: Valpak Scotland

What Valpak Scotland is responsible for

On behalf of Tesco Ltd., Valpak Scotland is responsible for:

  • submitting packaging data to the environmental regulator
  • reporting organisation details and keeping them up to date
  • handling packaging recovery notes (PRNs) - these are evidence that packaging has been recycled, recovered or exported
  • paying scheme administrator costs and charges to the environmental regulator/li>

What Tesco Ltd. is responsible for

Tesco Ltd. is responsible for any waste management fee that may apply to it.
