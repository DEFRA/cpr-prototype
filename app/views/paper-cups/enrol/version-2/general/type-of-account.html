What is takeaway drinks seller

You’re a takeaway drinks seller if you fill drinks in takeaway cups and sell them from UK premises.

What is a takeback scheme

As a takeback scheme you support your members, who are takeaway drink sellers. You can support them to:

  • check if they need to report data on disposable cups
  • collect the cups from your premises
  • weigh the cups
  • arrange the recycling
  • report the data
