Paying your registration fees

You must pay all your fees before you can submit your registration.

As a takeback scheme there are 2 types of annual fees you must pay.

Takeback Scheme fee

This is your registration fee. You must pay this to the environmental regulator for each nation you and your members operate in.

Members registration fee

You must pay this annual fee for your members. This must be paid to every environmental regulator where your members have premises.
