Meeting your packaging obligation

To meet your 2025 packaging obligation of 18,000 tonnes, you still need to:

  1. Buy PRNs or PERNs for your packaging materials

    To meet your recycling obligations, you must purchase PRNs from an accredited reprocessor or exporter. This certifies that the packaging waste has been properly recycled or exported for recycling.

    See the public register of accredited reprocessors and exporters

  2. Accept any outstanding PRNs or PERNs to meet your material obligations

    Once you've purchased PRNs or PERNs, ensure they are accepted by your compliance scheme or regulatory authority. This will help you meet your material obligations and avoid any compliance issues.
