What to do next

To fulfil your 2025 packaging obligation of 18,000 tonnes, you need to:

  • Buy Packaging Recycling Notes (PRNs) or Packaging Export Recycling Notes (PERNs) from an accredited reprocessor or exporter. This demonstrates that packaging waste has been recycled or exported for recycling.
  • Accept any outstanding PRNs or PERNs to meet your material obligations. Once you've purchased PRNs or PERNs, you’ll need to get them accepted by an accreditated reprocessor, exporter or compliance scheme. This is a crucial step in demonstrating compliance.
  • Track the status of your obligation. Make sure all your purchased PRNs and PERNs are correctly recorded, accepted and accounted for to avoid any compliance issues.

Once you fully meet your obligation for each material, you’ll be issued a certificate to demonstrate full compliance.

For more information on buying and accepting PRNs and PERNs, visit the GOV.UK guidance on extended producer responsibility (EPR) for packaging. (opens in new tab).
