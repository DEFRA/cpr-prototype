How we calculated your obligation

Total glass obligation

We applied this formula to calculate your total glass obligation:

L x Y = total glass obligation

  • L is 33,784 tonnes (the total glass material you reported for 2024 to the nearest tonne)
  • Y is 74% (the 2025 recycling target for glass)

Your total glass recycling obligation is:
33,784 tonnes x 74% = 25,000 tonnes.

Glass re-melt obligation

Your glass re-melt obligation is part of your total glass obligation. We used the formula set out in the 2025 regulations to calculate it:

T x (L x Y) = G

  • T is 75% (the 2025 percentage recycling target for glass re-melt)
  • L is 33,784 tonnes (the total glass material you reported for 2024 to the nearest tonne)
  • Y is 74% (the 2025 percentage recycling target for glass)

Your glass re-melt obligation (G) is:
75% x (33,784 tonnes x 74%) = 18,000 tonnes.

Remaining glass obligation

To determine your remaining glass obligation, we subtract the glass re-melt obligation from your total obligation.

Your remaining glass obligation is:
25,000 tonnes - 18,000 tonnes = 7,000 tonnes.
