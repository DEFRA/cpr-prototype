Supplying goods under your own brand

This is when you place goods labelled with your brand onto the UK market.

A brand includes any of the following:

  • a logo
  • a trademark
  • any distinctive mark

You supply goods under your own brand even if you pay another company to carry out part of the supply chain for you. For example, you may pay another organisation to do any of the following:

  • produce goods that will be sold under your brand name
  • pack goods that will be sold under your brand name
  • place your branded goods on the UK market
