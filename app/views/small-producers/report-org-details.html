Report your organisation details

Before you start - read the guidance

Read the guidance on how to report organisation details (opens in a new window). There are template documents you can use to build your files.

What you need to do

  1. Upload your members’ organisation details to your account in a CSV file.
  2. We'll tell you if you also need to upload files with brand and partner information.
  3. Submit your members’ details to the environmental regulator by the deadline. Only people with the right account permission can do this.

Make sure you have all the files ready

Have all the files ready before you start, including the brand and partner information if you need them.
