Report your organisation details

Before you start

Read the guidance on how to report organisation details (opens in a new window).
There are template documents you can use to build your files.

What you need to do

  1. Upload organisation details to your account in a CSV file. We’ll check the data is in the right format.
  2. Based on what is in that file, you may also need to upload files with brand and partner information.
  3. Submit the data to the environmental regulator by the deadline - only people with the right account permission can do this.

You can upload new files to your account at any time before you submit.
