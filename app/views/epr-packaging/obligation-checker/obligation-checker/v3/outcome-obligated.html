You will need to take action under the extended producer responsibility for packaging regulations

What this means for your organisation

You are classed as a small organisation. To comply with the regulations you must register, submit data, and pay charges.

Register

Register for the EPR for packaging online service when registration opens for small organisations in 2024. You’ll can create an account before registration opens.

You’ll need to pay a registration fee.

Submit data

You must:

  • take steps to record the data about all the packaging you handle and supply through the UK market from 1 January 2023
  • submit data about the packaging and packaged goods you handled and supplied through the UK market throughout 2023 (submissions open in January 2024 and close on 1 April 2024)

Pay charges

You do not need to pay a waste management charge.

Your next steps

  1. Prepare to collect data about the packaging you handle or supply in 2023. Find out more about the data you'll need to collect.
  2. Create an account on behalf on your organisation. Once you have created an account, you’ll receive email updates about your next steps.
