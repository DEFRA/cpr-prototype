Check if you need to register for Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) for packaging

Use this service to find out if your organisation will need to:

  • register for EPR for packaging
  • submit data about how you work wth packaging
  • pay charges to cover the recovery, disposal and recycling cost of the packaging you produce

To use this service you'll need to know:

  • your annual turnover
  • how your organisation works with packaging
  • how much packaging your organisation works with
It should take about 5 minutes to check.
