Check if your business is obligated

There are two schemes that form part of the Collection and Packaging Reforms, your business may be obligated for either scheme if they meet certain criteria.

Extended Producer Responsibility obligation criteria

You’re an ‘obligated’ packaging producer if you meet both of the following criteria. You:

  • handled 25 tonnes of packaging materials or packaging in the previous calendar year
  • have a turnover of more than £1 million a year (based on the last financial year’s accounts)

Deposit Return Scheme obligation criteria

You’re ‘obligated’ for the deposit return scheme if you meet any of the following criteria. You:

  • fill drinks containers with your own branded drinks to place on the market in England, Wales or Northern Ireland
  • import filled drinks containers from outside of the UK to place on the market in England, Wales or Northern Ireland
  • sell filled drinks containers to end consumers in England, Wales or Northern Ireland
