Upload packaging data to your account

Upload your packaging data in a CSV file. The file must be smaller than 256MB.

Once we have validated the data, we store the file on your account. If there’s a problem, we reject the upload and tell you about:

  • errors – you’ll need to fix all errors and re-upload your file
  • warnings – these are unusual entries you’ll need to check, like very low or high packaging weights, but they will not prevent your file from being submitted to the environmental regulator

It may take a few minutes to upload.

Do not close the window while the file is uploading.
