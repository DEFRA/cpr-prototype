A type 2 producer is an organisation that does at least one of the following:

  • imports filled packaging to sell in the UK
  • imports unfilled packaging to sell to a non-obligated producer in the UK

Non-obligated producers are organisations that either:

  • produce less than 25 tonnes of packaging waste a year
