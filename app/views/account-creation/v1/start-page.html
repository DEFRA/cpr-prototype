Set up an extended producer responsibility (EPR) for packaging account

This service is for organisations and sole traders that must take action to comply with the extended producer responsibility (EPR) for packaging regulations.

Use this service to:

  • create a Government Gateway account linked to your organisation (if you don't have one)
  • create a Defra account (if your organisation doesn't have one)
  • get access to the EPR digital service

What you'll need

To create a Government Gateway account, you'll need to tell us your email address and full name.

To create your Defra account, you'll need to tell us:

  • if your organisation trades in the UK
  • your organisation's Companies House number (if it has one)
  • your full name
  • your telephone number
  • your organisation's address
  • your organisation's main telephone number and email address (optional)

If you use this service to create a Defra account, you'll need your Government Gateway user ID and password to sign in and create your EPR for packaging account.
