Did your organisation place any secondary packaging on the UK market in 2022?

Secondary packaging is used to group a number of ‘sales units’. Organisations sometimes use it to display products in shops or sell it to the end consumer along with the products it contains.

{{ govukDetails({ summaryText: "View examples of secondary packaging", html: '

Example A

A baby food manufacturer sells individual baby food pouches grouped together in a cardboard tray and plastic wrap to a retail organisation. The retail organisation removes and disregards the plastic wrap. It puts the baby food pouches on shelves in the cardboard trays.

The secondary packaging is the cardboard tray and the plastic wrap.

Example B

An organisation imports computer parts into the UK. The computer parts are grouped together in a cardboard tray and box.

The secondary packaging is the cardboard tray and box it is transported in.
